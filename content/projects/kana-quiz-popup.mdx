# Kana Quiz Popup

A lightweight Japanese vocabulary learning tool that lives in your browser. Practice hiragana, katakana, and vocabulary with quick popup quizzes — perfect for learning on the side while working.

## Why I Built This

Learning Japanese alongside a full-time engineering job is hard. I wanted something that would pop up periodically and quiz me on kana characters without breaking my workflow. So I built it.

## Features

- Popup-style quiz that appears in the browser
- Covers hiragana, katakana, and vocabulary
- Lightweight — no heavy frameworks, no bloat
- Deployed at [study.truongha.com](https://study.truongha.com/)

## Tech

Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, CSS — kept intentionally simple to focus on the learning experience rather than the tech stack.
